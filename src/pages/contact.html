Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

What is your typical timeline for completing a project?

+

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple landing page might take 1-2 weeks, while a complex web application could take 2-3 months. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

What is your pricing structure?

+

I offer both fixed-price project rates and hourly billing depending on the nature of the work. For most client projects, I prefer a fixed-price approach based on clear requirements. This provides you with budget certainty. I'm happy to provide a detailed quote after understanding your project needs.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

+

Yes, I offer maintenance packages for websites and applications I've built. These typically include regular updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and content updates. We can discuss a maintenance plan that suits your needs once your project is complete.

What information do you need to start a project?

+

To get started, I'll need a clear description of your project goals, target audience, desired features, and any design preferences. Examples of websites or applications you like can also be helpful. The more detailed information you provide, the more accurate my initial proposal will be.
